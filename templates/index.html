✅
Normal
Systolic <120 AND Diastolic <80 mmHg
Blood pressure is in the healthy range. Maintain your lifestyle with regular exercise, a balanced diet low in sodium and saturated fat, and avoid smoking. Schedule a BP check at least once every 1–2 years.
📅 Annual checkup recommended
⚠️
Elevated
Systolic 120–129 AND Diastolic <80 mmHg
BP is above normal but not yet hypertension. Without lifestyle changes, elevated BP is likely to develop into hypertension. Reduce sodium intake below 2,300 mg/day, increase aerobic activity to 150 min/week, limit alcohol, and track your BP weekly.
📅 Recheck in 3 months
🔴
Stage 1 Hypertension
Systolic 130–139 OR Diastolic 80–89 mmHg
This is confirmed hypertension. Consult a physician within one month. Adopt the DASH diet, reduce sodium below 1,500 mg/day, quit smoking, exercise regularly, and manage stress. Medication may be recommended depending on cardiovascular risk factors.
📅 See doctor within 1 month
🚨
Stage 2 Hypertension
Systolic ≥140 OR Diastolic ≥90 mmHg
This is severe hypertension and carries a significant risk of heart attack, stroke, and kidney damage. See a physician as soon as possible — this week. Antihypertensive medication is almost always required alongside aggressive lifestyle modifications.
📅 Seek medical care this week
